Living the Dream

Some words about why I've chosen Chas Academy

Just a few years ago, I thought that the IT sector wasn't really for me, but I challenged myself to learn more about it, and I feel lucky to have found something I enjoy this much!

I want to further move towards fullstack developement because I want to feel more confident in my skills, and I think an YH like Chas Academy can give me that. I also hope it will be a creative environment, where I can learn from my fellow students. I thrive in social environments, so working with developement in a team is an important part of my dream for the future.

And something about my dream profession

Another part of my dream is being able to use my previous experiences as a graphic designer in my work. That could mean working closely with the design team, developing their visions into working programmes.

A role that currently interests me is working as a consultant. I imagine it would give me the chance to tackle diverse projects, continuously learn, and use both my programming skills and design experience to create real value for clients. Ultimately, I want a position where I can grow, contribute creatively, and be part of a team that values collaboration and innovation. Also, rainy days can be nice.

Here's a company I think seems like it could be a nice work place

Friday is a consultant and recruitment agency with offices in Gothenburg, Stockholm, Linköping, Örebro and Malmö. They focus on matching junior talent in IT and tech with the right companies, aiming to make people love going to work every day.
